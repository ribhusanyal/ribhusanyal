About Ribhu Sanyal

Ribhu Sanyal (born 1983) is an upcoming Dhrupad singer. His interest in music grew from childhood by frequently listening to Late Ustad Zia Fariduddin Dagar. Music was always his passion and home discipline. He started formal training in Dhrupad from his father and Dhrupad exponent Pandit Ritwik Sanyal from a very early age of 8 years and along with his education, he continued his training and gradually became a natural Dhrupad singer.

Along with his Software Engineering studies, he successfully completed a senior diploma in vocal music from the Faculty of Performing Arts, Banaras Hindu University, Varanasi, and a graduate diploma from Prayag Sangeet Samiti, Allahabad. Along with his academic studies, he continued his practice of Alap, Dhrupad, and Dhamar genre of Indian classical music with his father from time to time.

Currently, he resides in Bangalore, India, as a Project Manager by vocation, but he is continuing his musical journey, performing all over India as a Dhrupad soloist and also as a junior partner of his illustrious father in prominent concert platforms in India.
