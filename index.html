Ribhu Sanyal

About

Ribhu Sanyal (born 1983) is an upcoming Dhrupad Singer. His interest in music grew from childhood by frequently hearing to Late Ustad Zia Fariduddin Dagar. Music was always his passion and home discipline. He started formal training in Dhrupad from his father and dhrupad exponent Pandit Ritwik Sanyal from very early age of 8 years and along with his education he continued his training and gradually became a natural Dhrupad singer.

Along with his Software engineering studies he successfully completed senior diploma in vocal music from Faculty of performing Arts, Banaras Hindu University, Varanasi and a graduate diploma from Prayag Sangeet Samiti, Allahabad. Along with his academic studies he continued his practice of Alap Dhrupad and Dhamar genre of Indian classical music with his father from time to time.

Currently he resides in Bangalore, India as a Project Manager by vocation but he is continuing his musical journey performing all over India as Dhrupad soloist and also as a junior partner of his illustrious father in prominent concert platforms in India.
